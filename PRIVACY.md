# Privacy Policy for Word Hacker 404

**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

## Overview
Word Hacker 404 is a desktop application that respects your privacy. This document explains how we handle data.

## Data Collection
- **We do NOT collect personal information**
- **We do NOT track your downloads**
- **We do NOT send your data to any server**

## Local Storage Only
- All downloads are saved locally on your computer
- No cloud storage or remote backups
- You have full control of your files

## Network Usage
- The app connects to Instagram to download videos (required functionality)
- The app connects to our Cloudflare Worker proxy (only for download processing)
- No user tracking or analytics

## Third-Party Services
- **Instagram:** Videos are downloaded from Instagram's public URLs
- **Cloudflare Worker:** Used as a proxy to improve download success rates
- Neither service receives personal information from our app

## Your Rights
- All your data stays on your device
- You can delete the app and all data anytime
- No account required, no registration

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We will update this policy if our data practices change. Check this page for updates.

## Contact
For privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub: https://github.com/Pramsss108/word-hacker-404/issues
